Economic Outcome of Long Term Stock Positions

Economic Outcome

What Happens when you Trade Long Term?

    for example:
  • A person who decides to get into trading on a long term scale can expect slow, but steady income. The best move is to buy fee free ETF's. A certain ammount each paycheck. THis will beat the market long term by about 3 percent. Better than any bank account.
  • It is not without its risks though. Say you bought $SPY, an ETF that is made up of the 500 biggest traded companies on the NYSE. And one of those pesky pandemic hits, and your stock tanks. That cannot happen in a savings account.

Societal Outcomes

What can society expect?

  • When someone trades long term they are stimulating the economy. If they purchase $SPY, they are subsequentally funding the 500 biggest companies in the US. A neccessity for companies to function and innovate.
  • If the companies were to fail and a person were to lose their money, this could hurt their personal life. Therefore, putting stress on various safetynets in society. A broke person with no hope will not be good for society.

Political Outcomes

How are politics in play here?.

  • As many of us know, polotics and the stock market are intertwined. Companies you invest in may use that money to lobby politicians in their interests. Also, a good stock market mean happy people and contenment with leadership. A bad economy leads to change in the people in power.
